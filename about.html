About me

Hello, I'm Anastasia!
I am a Ukrainian actress and photographer from Kyiv, based in Berlin.
I have been engaged in genre, portrait and still life photography. I love still life photography with natural lighting. I am interested in revealing the personality as much as possible, so it is important for me to find contact with the model, observe her and emphasize her features. I also really like to shoot children (I find contact with them very easily), couples in love and make reportage shots of important life events.
